Basil Williams was one of three brothers, he was the oldest, his Brother Ralph Williams was also a cyclist the father of Michael Williams. Basil was a Champion on the Road and track. These cyclist were in the forefront of where the sport originated from. They could not compete at the highest level due to apartheid. These are our struggle hero's.
